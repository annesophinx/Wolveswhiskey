Whiskey

Spring Run is a variation of Wolves signature blend, consisting of whiskey distilled from Stout craft beer, aged in French Oak for 10 years, whiskey distilled from Pilsner beer, aged in New American Oak for 6 years, a Rye selected for its back palate spice, and a single malt American whiskey distilled from two-row malted barley and aged in used French Oak for 10 years.

Nose: Honeysuckle, citrus, hops, black cherry, warm vanilla.

Tasting Notes: Tangerine, dark chocolate, hops, oak, citrus, nutmeg.

Finish: Long and warm on the front pallet, rising space on the finish.

Special Projects

The Rolce Royce

There is no more finely detailed, bespoke automobile in the world than Rolls-Royce. Wolves has partnered with Rolls on the rollout of the Black Badge edition of the Rolls Royce Ghost. Working with Rolls, Wolves designed and produced a run of whiskey titled “The Black Badge” to celebrate the release of one of the finest automobiles in the world, and will be pouring the whiskey at private Rolls-Royce events around North America. The collaboration is a celebration of bespoke, handmade product.

The Hotel Series

Wolves is launching the The Hotel Series, which are whiskey collaborations with some of the world’s finest hotels. The first release, titled Table 9, is with the Peninsula Beverly Hills. Table 9 is a variation of Wolves signature blend, with a high-pilsner content, and is named after the most sought after table in the hotel. In celebration of the partnership, Michelin-starred chef of The Peninsula, Ralf Schlegel, prepared a private, four-course dinner experience at the hotel, with each course paired with a different Wolves expression.



2/3

The Quail

Wolves was invited to participate at the Quail, A Motorsports Gathering – the pinnacle of automotive excellence. Our tasting experience was next to the classic Ferrari collection. For the event, we produced 250 bottles of our signature blend, titled “The Quail Run,” and wrapped each bottle in the same Napa leather used by Porsche in their interiors. Wolves was also tapped to create an award for Best of Show at the Quail 2021. For this, we ripped the seat out of a rare 1974 Porsche Carrera RS “Ducktail,” made a label from the leather, and wrapped it around a one-of-one 24 year aged California brandy.

Our Story

Wolves is American whiskey made in California from an equally creative group of founders, also based in California. Wolves is distilled in Sonoma County by 13th generation Master Distiller Marko Karakasevic using an old, slow, brandy still that was imported from Cognac in 1983. Uniquely, Wolves signature blend consists of whiskey created from refined craft beer, and Rye whiskey. The flavor profile is unlike anything else on the market.
